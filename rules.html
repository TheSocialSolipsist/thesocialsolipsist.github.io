Rules of Conduct

These are the rules for engaging in this community.
They are strictly enforced for the enjoyment and safety of everyone.
If you cannot match the energy of the stream or follow the rules, you will be removed.

  1. Be kind, and treat everyone with respect. Zero tolerance for harassment, bigotry, prejudice, or discrimination.
  2. Do not backseat. This includes ANY comment that could influence antipation, expectations, or game knowledge.
  3. Do not tell the streamer how to play, what to play, or make any unsolicited "recommendations" to the streamer.
  4. Do not raise new topics not already in discussion by the streamer.
  5. Do not discuss other streamers or anyone not currently being spoken about by the streamer.
  6. Do not discuss controvertial content. This includes politics, religion, current events, or distressing subjects.
  7. Do not make atttention-seeking comments. No vaugeposting, demanding reponses, or advertising yourself or others.
  8. Do not reveal or discuss identifying information about yourself or others.
  9. Do not argue with mods or moderation decisions.
