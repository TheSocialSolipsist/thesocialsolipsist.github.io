Rules of Conduct

These are the rules for engaging in this community. They are strictly enforced for the enjoyment and safety of everyone.

If you try to skirt the edge on a technicality, you will be banned extra hard.

  1. Treat everyone with respect, regardless, of their identity, status, or qualities. Harassment, bigotry, prejudice, and discrimination will never be tolerated.
  2. Absolutely no backseating. This includes ANY comments that influence expectations or knowledge. No "hints" or "did you know" or "I love this part".
  3. Stay on topic. Do not raise new topics not already in discussion by the streamer. Side conversations should be taken elsewhere.
  4. Do not discuss other streamers, personalities, public figures, or any individuals not currently being spoken about by the streamer.
  5. Do not discuss controvertial content. This includes politics, religion, current events, or anything else that might spark heated debate.
  6. Attention-seeking behavior will result in an instant ban. This includes both trying to draw undue personal attention, or trying to advertise yourself or others.
  7. Do not reveal or discuss identifying information about yourself or others.
